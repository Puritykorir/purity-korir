About

Since our founding in 2015, the University of Mahanaim has been a hub for learning, innovation, problem solving and community building. Driven by a mission to serve the greater good, our students, faculty and staff tackle today’s most pressing challenges with courage and creativity, making a difference across Kenyan state — and around the world.

Our campus

The Mahanaim University comprises campuses in Kenya, Korea and China, as well as a world-class medical center serving the states and the regions. Each year, the Mahanaim University inspires more than 60,000 students to learn from renowned experts in their fields. As a “best buy” international university, our goal is to make higher education accessible for all.

We’re committed to excellence in training the next generation of health professionals and researchers. And with over 1 million cancer patient visits and $729 million in uncompensated care for low-income patients in need in 2021, we are dedicated to the health and well-being of Kenyans nationwide.
